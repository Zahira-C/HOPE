H.O.P.E.

Welcome to H.O.P.E.! The purpose of this app is to act as a resource for those suffering from depression and inform the general public about depression. We hope this may be an accessible tool for you.

Project Semicolon

Project Semicolon works to provide those suffering from mental illness with suicidal thoughts to a caring community and a wider access to resources.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

This the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline website's. If you or a loved one you know are thinking about suicide, call at 1-800-273-8255. They are available 24 hours a day.
